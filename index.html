Features

Everything in one place

Café, grocery, supplier, courier — whatever your business model.

Lightning-Fast Sales

Scan a barcode and the product is added instantly. Cart → payment → receipt: 3 seconds.

Never Stops

İnternet kesilse, uygulama 7 güne kadar kesintisiz çalışır. Bağlantı geri geldiğinde her şey otomatik olarak sunucuyla yedeklenir.

Z

Z-Report & Analytics

Daily close, monthly summary, category analysis — all with charts.

Freelance Courier

Broadcast model. Post a request, the nearest courier accepts. Real-time tracking.

Supplier System

Place an order, receive it — the whole process with your supplier inside the app.

24 Languages & RTL

Turkish, English, Arabic, Persian and 21 more. Full RTL layout support.

Pruva Occasion

Built-in consignment & second-hand module. Sellers submit photos → AI fills name, condition and price → shop owner approves. Commission splits automatically.

Customer Credit

Track buy-now-pay-later balances for regulars. Full audit trail, stored locally — never uploaded without your permission.

Blind Supplier Data

Suppliers see only a depletion band (0–100%) — never your actual stock count. Your inventory stays private while suppliers still know when to reorder.

Shrinkage & Loss Report

Compare expected vs. physical count shelf by shelf. Instant loss value. Catch theft or breakage before it compounds.

AppLock — Zero-Trust Security

PIN + biometric with exponential brute-force backoff. Clock-manipulation resistant. Keys stored in Keychain, never in UserDefaults.

Kitchen Display (KDS)

Orders flow from table to kitchen in real time. Chef updates status — waiter gets a push notification the moment the dish is ready.
